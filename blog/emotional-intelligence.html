What the heck is EQ and why the heck should you care?

21 January 2025

What is EQ?

At its core, EQ is the ability to recognise, understand, and manage your emotions, and also understand how others are feeling. It involves five main things:

  1. Self-awareness - Knowing how you feel and why.
  2. Self-regulation -Keeping your emotions in check, especially in stressful situations.
  3. Motivation - Having an inner drive to achieve goals and stay focused.
  4. Empathy - Understanding how others feel, even when they don't say it.
  5. Social skills - Building good relationships and working well with others.

How is EQ different from IQ?

EQ, on the other hand, is about handling emotions and navigating social situations. While IQ helps you solve problems, EQ helps you connect with people, manage stress, and lead teams.

Why is EQ important?

Having high EQ can significantly improve your relationships, as it allows you to understand and connect with others on a deeper level. It also helps you stay calm and manage stress better when things get hectic. People with strong EQ make great leaders because they can motivate their teams and understand their needs, while also resolving conflicts in a calm, constructive way. In short, EQ is a game-changer - it helps you build stronger relationships, succeed at work, and feel more in control of your emotions.
